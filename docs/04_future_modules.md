# Future Modules & Expansions

*This document captures ideas for future tools and features to be integrated into the Naut ERP platform once the core modules are established.*

## Ideas

### 1. Engineering Calculators
- Dynamic payload calculation tools.
- Orbital trajectory or endurance mapping tools for hardware deployment.

### 2. HR & Provider Portal
- Dedicated login for field agents/cleaners/technicians to view their schedule.
- Time-tracking integration that feeds directly into the Finance module for payroll.

### 3. Client Facing Portal
- A secure area where clients can review their service history, download invoices, and request new bookings.

### 4. Advanced Analytics & AI
- Predictive stock depletion alerts.
- AI-driven routing for logistics efficiency.
